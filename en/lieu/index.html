The place & the story

Where time stretches a little.

Inspired by Mediterranean settings, sun-baked terraces and walled gardens, Cofy Corner is a corner of warmth at the heart of the Tonneliers district.

The craft

The rigour of the gesture.

Hand-selected beans, roasted on site. A precise dose, a clean extraction, a brew timed to the second. The context is sensed more than it is shown.

The corner's hand

Run by people, not machines.

Behind the counter, a small team that knows your order. You recognise a Cofy at a glance — by the apron, as much as by the way they greet you.
